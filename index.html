        About Alya

        Alya was created as a student project at Bar-Ilan University as part of a Product Management course.

        Our mission is to make lone soldier benefits accessible and understandable for every IDF lone soldier and new immigrant (oleh), regardless of their background or Hebrew level.

        The 3 Tools

        Rights & Grants — Answer 4 quick questions and get a personalized list of benefits you likely qualify for, plus the documents you need to apply.

        AI Mentor — A supportive chat for navigating army slang, bureaucracy, and the everyday challenges of lone service. For urgent welfare issues, the mentor will always direct you to your Mashakit Tash or ERAN (1201).

        Lease Shield — Paste your rental contract and the AI flags risky clauses and suggests safer wording.

        Disclaimer

        Alya is an early-stage AI prototype. It does not replace official legal or military advice. Always confirm eligibility and rights with your commanding officer, welfare NCO (Mashakit Tash), or the relevant authority.

        Privacy Policy

        What data we collect

        Alya does not store any personal information you enter. Your inputs (profile answers, chat messages, contract text) are sent to OpenAI's API in real time to generate a response, then discarded.

        OpenAI

        Responses are powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o mini model. Content sent through the API is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not use your data to train models.

        What you should not enter

        Do not enter your full military ID number, classified information, or other highly sensitive details. Use the tools for general guidance only.

        Cookies & Analytics

        Alya does not use cookies or analytics trackers. No personal data is collected about your browsing behavior.
